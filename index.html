My cosmic experiences

After a day which almost makes me feel like a failure,the night sky becomes my most comfort shelter. It shares it's stories to me that how after every destruction it created itself and became more stronger. It becomes my friend who listens to me and my stories.

The night sky feels like home where the stars are holding my childhood stories. I am obsessed with it's beauty.

In all these beautiful things the most beautiful one for me is my moon. I can't get over the moment when I saw it for the first time through telescope. My world paused. There were creaters, small-big which made it so beautiful. It was perfectly imperfect. I feel blessed to carry my childhood fantasy still with me, but now it's not just a fantasy-it's a reality.
